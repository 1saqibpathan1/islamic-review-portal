Ji.

Jaise ke, humare ahle hadees mein, jaise ke, taraweeh ki namaz 8 rakat ki hoti hai, to doosre masajid mein jo hai na 20 rakat ki rehti hai. Jaise wo imam ke sath agar hum 8 rakat parhne ke baad, to wahan se alag ho sakte hain? Witr namaz alag parh sakte hain?

Haan, wahan se aap 8 rakat parh ke ya to alag ho jayein, aur agar aap witr parhna chahte hain to un ke sath phir, jo hai nauvin aur dasvin rakat parhiye, aur gyarvi rakat parh ke phir aap alag ho jaiye. Matlab teen rakat witr parh ke phir alag ho jaiye. Aur aakhir ki jo aap ki teen rakat hon us mein witr ki niyat kijiye, us mein shafa ki niyat na kijiye. Ye tareeqa ye hai, doosra tareeqa ye hai ke 8 parh ke aap alag ho jaiye, jaiye apne ghar ya masjid mein kahin aur ja ke aap witr parh lijiye. Samajh mein aayi baat bhai?

Shaikh agar is... haan, shaikh agar maan ke chalein is masjid ke andar ek imam imamat kar rahe hain, namaz parha rahe hain, is masjid mein agar hum alag se witr parhenge to kya ho jayega?

Haan bilkul ho jayega, kyunki wo log jo hain sunnat ki mukhalifat kar rahe hain 20 rakat parh ke, is liye is mukhalifat mein aap sath nahi denge. 8 rakat tak aap parhiye, us ke baad witr aap alag bhi parh sakte hain, aur agar chahen to un ke sath aap witr parhein lekin witr ki niyat kijiye 8 ke baad. Aur jab aap ki teen rakat ho jayein to phir aap alag ho jaiye imam se aur tashahhud kar ke salaam pher dijiye.

Theek hai shaikh, jazakallah khair, alhamdulillah.
