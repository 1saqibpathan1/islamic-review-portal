# Q&A Transcript

**Question:** Sheikh, agar ek nabaligh larki, matlab jiski umar 10 saal ho gayi ho, kya us ke liye burqa dalna zaroori hai, baaligh na hone ke bawajood bhi?

**Answer:** Burqe ka ta'alluq fitne se hai. Jis tarah se hamara samaj bimar ho chuka hai, aurton se muta'alliq, is samaj mein aap yeh na dekhein ke jab woh baaligh ho jayegi tab burqa pehnayenge. Is bimar samaj mein jab bachi itni badi ho gayi ke ab woh fitne ka sabab ban sakti hai yeh cheez, to us ko burqa pehnayein.
